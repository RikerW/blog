10 Days of Sun

Challenge proposal: Spend at least 45 minutes in the sun every day

I spend most of my time on the computer every day. Like, I spend probably easily 6+ hours on a good day.

Personally, I have no real issues with that. I'm mostly doing useful or at least interesting things (in my opinion)

However, my mother has requested multiple times that I spend less time on the computer. So here's my attempt at that.


Day 1 (5/20)

I've started walking around the pool for 45-60 minutes a day. It's reasonably good exercise, and it's pretty simple.

I'm mostly just reading books as I walk. I've gotten reasonably good at it now (after 2 hours of practice).

I can do laps around the pool while reading and not really have to think about it. It's amusing.
